Our Competition Team Program

The competition team at Sam's Dance Center is an opportunity of a lifetime. They will get the opportunity to train with the best in the industry every week. The members of the team become a family that works together, trains together, and competes together. All members get the chance to compete against other dance studios during the season that are close to home and against national competitiors at Nationals every year.

The competition team is a serious commitment, but if you are dedicated it is an amazing experience. You can schedule a private audition by calling us at (631)581*2947.

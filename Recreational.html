Our Recreational Team Program

The recreational dance program at Sam's Dance Center is available to anyone who wants to dance and enjoys dancing. This program is much less of a commitment, as classes only meet once a week. However, they will expand their knowledge and continue to grow.

These dancers will perform in our annual recital that can be attended by the entire family. It is a great way to make friends that last a lifetime and enjoy the week.
